Smartphone Z1

The Smartphone Z1 features a stunning 6.5-inch OLED display, 48MP dual cameras, and a powerful octa-core processor. Experience lightning-fast 5G connectivity and an all-day battery life designed for professionals and enthusiasts alike.

Additional features include facial recognition, wireless charging, and water resistance, making it a versatile and durable choice for modern users.

Laptop Z2

The Laptop Z2 boasts a 16-inch Retina display, next-gen SSD storage, and extended battery life for all your productivity needs. Powered by the latest generation processors, it is ideal for creatives, developers, and business users seeking performance and portability.

Enjoy a sleek aluminum chassis, advanced cooling system, and comprehensive connectivity options including Thunderbolt 4 ports.

Smart Watch Z3

Stay connected and monitor your health with the Smart Watch Z3. Featuring a vibrant AMOLED screen, advanced health tracking sensors, GPS, and up to 7 days of battery life, it is designed for fitness enthusiasts and tech-savvy users alike.

Compatible with both Android and iOS platforms, the Smart Watch Z3 offers customizable watch faces and a range of fitness apps.

Wireless Headphones Z4

The Wireless Headphones Z4 deliver crystal-clear audio with active noise cancellation and a comfortable ergonomic design. Enjoy up to 30 hours of playback on a single charge, and seamless Bluetooth connectivity for both work and leisure.

Additional features include touch controls, quick charging, and voice assistant integration.

Smart Home Hub Z5

The Smart Home Hub Z5 integrates all your smart devices into a single, easy-to-control platform. Featuring voice control compatibility, customizable automation, and robust security features, it is the cornerstone of your connected living space.

Control lighting, climate, security cameras, and more with intuitive apps and voice assistants.
